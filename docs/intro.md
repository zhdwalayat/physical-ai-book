# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

> Bridging Digital Intelligence and Physical Embodiment

## Welcome

This comprehensive textbook teaches you to design, simulate, and deploy humanoid robots capable of natural human interactions using **ROS 2**, **Gazebo**, and **NVIDIA Isaac**.

Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence with physical embodiment—systems that don't merely process data but **act upon the world**, subject to gravity, friction, and the unpredictable complexity of real environments.

## What You'll Learn

By completing this course, you will be able to:

- **Understand** the principles of embodied intelligence
- **Design** humanoid robot systems using industry-standard tools
- **Simulate** robots in high-fidelity virtual environments
- **Deploy** AI systems capable of natural human interaction
- **Integrate** large language models with physical robot control

## Prerequisites

This book is **self-contained**. No prior robotics experience required. Basic programming knowledge (Python) is helpful but not mandatory.

## Case Studies

Throughout the book, we reference real-world humanoid robots:

- **Tesla Optimus** - General purpose manufacturing
- **Figure 01/02** - AI-native humanoid design
- **Boston Dynamics Atlas** - Advanced mobility
- **Unitree H1/G1** - Affordable research platforms
- **Agility Robotics Digit** - Commercial deployment
